About Us

Flames of India – A Culinary Voyage

Nestled in Southend-on-Sea, Flames of India serves authentic and flavourful Indian cuisine that captivates every food enthusiast. Each dish is freshly prepared using handpicked herbs and traditional spices, offering you the true essence of India’s rich culinary heritage.

Tasteful Choices

Explore a delightful variety of North and South Indian delicacies — each crafted with care and served at pleasantly affordable prices. A perfect value-for-money experience designed to please every palate.

Dining Experience

Immerse yourself in a cosy ambience designed for comfort and connection — whether it is a family dinner, a romantic evening, or a special celebration, Flames of India is your ultimate culinary haven.
